About Me

I am a MS student at Spatial Analysis and Modeling Laboratory (SAMLAB), Center for Ocean River Atmosphere and Land Sciences (CORAL), Indian Institute of Techonology kharagpur; pursuing my reaserch under the supervison of Prof. MD Behera.
